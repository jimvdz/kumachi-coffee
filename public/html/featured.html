Who we are

Kumachi is a local coffee shop that started as an online store on Facebook and decided to establish their physical store, which has now been operating for more than a month. Their objective and goals for the business is to let love delight in a person's heart through innovative coffee experience.

Did you know?

Kumachi’s mission and vision also came from the Bible verse 1 Corinthians 13:7, which describes ‘love as a safe place of shelter, for it never stops believing the best for others.’ Moreover, their brand represents a bear that holds a cup of coffee, as 'love bears all things'.

The Origin of Coffee
─────

Legend has it that coffee was discovered by a goat herder in Ethiopia who noticed his goats became unusually energetic after eating coffee berries. Since then, coffee has become one of the most popular beverages worldwide, with over 2.25 billion cups consumed daily!

The Health Benefits of Coffee
─────

Not only does coffee give you that much-needed energy boost, but it also comes with health benefits. Studies show that coffee is rich in antioxidants and can help reduce the risk of certain diseases, including Type 2 diabetes, Alzheimer's, and Parkinson’s.

The Art of Brewing
─────

Did you know there are over 15 ways to brew coffee? From the classic drip brew to the sophisticated pour-over or the robust French press, each method extracts unique flavors, offering endless possibilities for your perfect cup.

The Perfect Espresso Shot
─────

Crafting the perfect espresso is an art in itself, requiring precise pressure, water temperature, and grind size. A well-pulled espresso shot packs a powerful punch of flavor and intensity in just 1 ounce, with a rich crema layer on top that signifies a perfectly balanced brew.
